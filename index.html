Water Polo

Washington University is a part of the Heartland Conference. Our opponents include Augustana College, Carleton College, Grinnell College, Knox College, and St. Mary's University.

Practice starts Wednesday, September 2 for the 2015-2016 season. We practice Wednesday 8:30 pm - 10:30 pm and Sunday 3:00 pm - 6:00 pm through winter break. After winter break, the schedule will be Monday & Wednesday, 8:30 pm - 10:30 pm, and Saturday from 3:00 pm - 6:00 pm.

Facebook fan page is linked. For current players, you will be invited to the team page. All tournaments will be listed there when available, so you can confirm your availability to attend. Practice changes, team events, etc. will also be posted there.
